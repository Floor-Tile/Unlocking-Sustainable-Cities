About the Author

Paul Chatterton is a writer, researcher and campaigner. He is Professor of Urban Futures in the School of Geography. He is currently Director of the University's Sustainable Cities Group which has launched the ground breaking MSc Sustainable Cities [hyperlink: https://www.geog.leeds.ac.uk/study/masters/courses/msc-sustainable-cities/]. Paul is also co-founder and resident of the award winning low impact housing co-operative Lilac (www.lilac.coop). He has gone forward to help set up Leeds Commuity Homes to help promote community-led housing. His recent books include Low Impact Living (Routledge) (20% off with code DC361) and Unlocking Sustainable Cities with Pluto Press. His academic web page is at: https://www.geog.leeds.ac.uk/people/p.chatterton [The University of Leeds takes no responsibility for the content of www.unlockingsustainablecities.org]
